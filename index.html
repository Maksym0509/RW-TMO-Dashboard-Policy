Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Regan Wireless ("we," "our," or "us") operates the RW TMO Dashboard mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account and Authentication Information

When you log in to the App, we collect the following information:

  • Email address
  • Password (transmitted securely and not stored in plain text on the device)
  • Authentication tokens (JWT) stored securely on your device using encrypted storage

b. Business and Operational Data

The App displays business-related data retrieved from our servers, including:

  • Sales metrics, revenue, and growth data
  • Inventory and parts information
  • FMIP (Field Missing Inventory Program) submission statuses
  • Warehouse-related data

This data is associated with your organization and role. It is retrieved from our servers and is not collected from your personal device.

c. Device Information

We may automatically collect limited device information necessary for the App to function properly, such as:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide secure access to the App.
  • App Functionality: To display relevant business dashboards, metrics, charts, and reports based on your role and warehouse assignments.
  • Notifications: To alert you of pending items or updates that require your attention within the App.
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure the integrity of your account.
  • Improvements: To maintain, improve, and optimize the App's performance and user experience.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it:

  • Authentication tokens are stored on your device using encrypted secure storage (Expo SecureStore), which leverages the platform's native secure storage mechanisms (Keychain on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android).
  • All communication between the App and our servers is transmitted over HTTPS using encrypted connections.
  • Passwords are never stored in plain text on your device.
  • Session tokens are validated on each App launch, and expired sessions require re-authentication.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within the Organization: Your data may be accessible to authorized personnel within Regan Wireless and T-Mobile as part of normal business operations.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App and our servers, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Expo / EAS (Expo Application Services): Used for app building and distribution. Expo's privacy policy applies to their handling of data.

We do not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or social media tracking tools within the App.

6. Data Retention

We retain your authentication data only for as long as necessary to provide access to the App. Authentication tokens stored on your device are removed when you sign out. Business data displayed in the App is retrieved in real time from our servers and is not permanently stored on your device.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal or business retention requirements.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by discontinuing use of the App.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by authorized business personnel only and is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
